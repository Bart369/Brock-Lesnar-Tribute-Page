Brock "The Beast" Lesnar

One way ticket to Suplex City

Here's a timeline of Brock Lesnar's professional career highlights:

March 2002 - Brock's WWE debut. Runs from the crowd and in to the ring and beats up Al Snow, Maven and Spike Dudley. This was the best moment of Maven and Spike's career.

Brock standing over Maven, Spike on the turnbuckle!

Brock holding The Rock for the F5!!!

August 2002 - Defeats The Rock to win the WWE Championship for the first time. The Rock was totally over WWE and ready to make that big $$$ in Hollywood. The crowd could tell and boo'd him for it.

January 2003 - Wins the Royal Rumble by defeating the Undertaker. Heading to the main event at Wrestlemania 19!

Brock face to face with the Deadman.

Kurt Angle raises Brock's arm after his losing the match. Brock was concussed after messing up his jump off the turnbuckle.

March 2003 - Defeats Kurt Angle at Wrestlemania 19 to win his second WWE Championship. Nearly kills himself for trying to do a backflip off the turnbuckle and landing on his neck. You're not supposed to land on your neck.

February 2008 - Brock's UFC debut, loses to Frank Mir due to Round 1 Submission. Frank Mir surprised Brock as he was able to grab Brock's leg in the middle of getting his face bashed in. Learning experience for Brock.

Frank Mir surprises Brock with the submission.

Brock with the ground and pound on Randy Couture.

November 2008 - Win's the UFC Heavyweight Championship via TKO against Randy Couture in the second round. Brock was just to big and powerful for an older Randy.

July 2009 - Unified the UFC Heavyweight Championship by defeating Interim Champion Frank Mir via TKO in round 2. Sweet sweet revenge.

Brock holding down Frank Mir.

Brock flips off the crowd after they boo him for talking trash after beating Frank Mir. LOL.

Brock with the F5 on John Cena.

April 2012 - Returns to WWE by attacking John Cena. First of many up beatdowns Brock will be giving Cena in the upcoming years.

April 2014 - Brock shocked the world, ending the Undertaker's Wrestlemania winning streat at Wrestlemania 30. This was a really boring match.

A shocked Paul Heyman raising Brock's arm after his victory over the Undertaker.

Brock hits Cena with german suplexes all night.

August 2014 - Wins the WWE Championship by defeating John Cena at SummerSlam. Total squash match.

March 2015 - Suplex City opens it's doors. Brock yells out "Suplex City B*tch!" after tossing Roman Reigns around with a flurry of german suplexes at Wrestlemania 31. Nobody wants to go to Suplex City.

German suplex number 546893 on Roman Reigns.

Brock faces off against Mark Hunt.

July 2016 - Defeats Mark Hunt in his UFC return at UFC 200 by unanimous decision. Shoutout to the PEDs.
